# In the context of CompTIA Security+ training

This image showcases a product, specifically Faraday Cage EMP ESD Bags, which are used for protecting electronic devices from electromagnetic pulses (EMP) and other security threats. 

Physical Security Measures: Security+ includes topics about protecting physical hardware from environmental or intentional threats, such as electromagnetic interference or tampering.

Data Protection: The use of Faraday bags can help secure devices from wireless attacks, like RFID skimming or unwanted signals, aligning with the course's focus on securing sensitive data.

 This is another layer of protection for physical and electromagnetic security covered in cybersecurity principles.
