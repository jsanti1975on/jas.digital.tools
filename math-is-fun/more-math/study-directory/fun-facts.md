Continuing Education for CompTIA Certifications

URL Reference

CompTIA Continuing Education Units (CEUs)

Overview

The CompTIA Continuing Education (CE) Program helps certified individuals maintain their certifications by earning Continuing Education Units (CEUs). This process ensures skills remain current with evolving industry standards.

Key Information

Purpose: Maintain certification validity and ensure skills are up-to-date.

Applicable Certifications: Most CompTIA certifications that are part of the CE Program, including A+, Network+, Security+, and others.

Earning CEUs

Participants can earn CEUs by engaging in various activities such as:

Training and Education: Completing relevant courses or training sessions.

Certifications: Earning additional industry certifications.

Work Experience: Applying certified skills in the workplace.

Publishing Articles or Blogs: Sharing expertise through written contributions.

Webinars and Seminars: Attending approved industry events.

CompTIA Activities: Participating in activities like item writing or reviewing exam content.

Submission Process

CEUs are submitted through the CompTIA Certification Account, where users can log their activities and ensure they meet the requirements.

Important Notes

CEUs must be earned within the three-year renewal cycle.

A specified number of CEUs are required for each certification.

Fees may apply for renewal through the CE Program.

For more detailed information, visit the official CompTIA CEU page.
